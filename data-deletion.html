Data Deletion

Last updated: July 21, 2026

You can request deletion of your BuffBuddy account and all associated personal data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to admin@omninoodle.com with the subject line "Delete My Data" from the email address associated with your BuffBuddy account.

What Gets Deleted

Upon a verified request, we will permanently delete your account profile, workout logs, progress history, body metrics, and any data obtained through connected sign-in providers (such as Facebook or Google Sign-In), except where retention is required to comply with legal obligations.

Timeline

We will process your request and confirm completion by email within 30 days of receipt.

Questions

If you have any questions about this process, contact us at admin@omninoodle.com.
