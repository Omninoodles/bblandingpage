The problem with most apps

You're putting in the work.
But are you actually improving?

Most people who train consistently have no real answer to that question. They feel like they're working hard, but without tracking the numbers, there's no way to know if this week's you is stronger than last month's you.

BuffBuddy fixes that. Every time you train, your history is there. You'll see what you lifted last session, watch your strength trend upward over time, and see your personal bests stack up after each session.

01 / Logging

Logging a workout should be fast and effortless.

BuffBuddy is built around speed. Log your sets as you go, without breaking your focus or slowing down between exercises. The interface stays out of your way so you can stay in yours.

You can also pull up what you did last session for any exercise while you're training. So when you're loading the bar, you know exactly what number you're chasing.

Log sets and reps as you go, one tap at a time
See your previous session for any exercise mid-workout
Build and save as many custom workout plans as you need
02 / Progress

See exactly how your strength is trending.

After each session, BuffBuddy updates your personal records for every exercise you logged. Weight PRs, rep PRs, tracked automatically so you never have to wonder where you stand.

Pull up any exercise and see your performance over time as a chart. Two months of progress laid out visually is motivating in a way that a list of numbers never is.

Personal records tracked per exercise, updated after every session
Visual progress charts for any exercise in your history
Your full workout history, searchable and filterable by date
